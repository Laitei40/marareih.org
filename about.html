About Mara Language Preservation

We are a grassroots, speaker-led initiative focused on protecting the Mara language and making it usable in modern digital environments.

Our Mission

Document Mara grammar and vocabulary, create open datasets, and provide tools so communities can use Mara on phones, computers, and the web.

Values

  • Open data and collaboration
  • Community-led development
  • Accessibility and respect for speakers
